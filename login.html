CTS fantasy WC '26

Company fantasy football game for World Cup 2026

Rules

Predict the final score of each World Cup 2026 match.

  • Exact score: 3 points
  • Correct winner/draw + correct goal difference: 2 points
  • Correct winner/draw only: 1 point
  • Wrong prediction: 0 points

Points are not cumulative. For each match, you receive only the highest applicable score.

You can enter or change your tip until 5 minutes before the match starts.

After each match is finished, tips are evaluated automatically and the scoreboard is updated.
